RIENVOR is a specialist practice built around the app-store rating layer: recovering, stabilising, and managing Play Store ratings, with monthly rating intelligence that keeps the read current.

Sameer Nehra founded RIENVOR. It works with early-stage and growth-stage businesses, and keeps every engagement under senior oversight rather than account management.

Where specialist execution is required, RIENVOR draws on a curated network of vetted operators, engaged selectively and coordinated under senior oversight. Accountability stays with the practice throughout.

Why I built RIENVOR

For years I watched good products lose installs to a number nobody owned. Teams owned acquisition, product, support, and growth, each of them measured and accountable. The rating sat in the middle of all of it. A drop of a few tenths of a star made every install more expensive, and still the number itself belonged to no one. The moment it fell below four stars, everyone called it a problem and no one was responsible for the fix.

I started RIENVOR to make that number someone's job. I've spent the years since on little else: getting the rating back to where installs convert, then holding it there while the product team does theirs. The practice stays small and senior because the rating rewards judgment over volume. Most of the job is knowing which complaints actually move the number and which only look like they should.

So the first conversation is never a pitch. It's a straight read on whether this is a problem I can move for you, and an honest answer if it isn't.

How we operate

RIENVOR functions as a boutique practice, not an agency. Engagements begin with a private diagnostic to establish fit and scope. Strategic direction comes from the founder; execution is handled directly or through curated partners, always under senior oversight.

01

Ratings Recovery & Management

Recovering, stabilising, and maintaining Play Store ratings — the rating layer treated as the unit of work, separate from the product itself.

02

Monthly Rating Intelligence

A monthly read of where the rating stands and what is moving it: rating health, complaint themes ranked by impact, and your position against comparable-scale apps.

03

In support of a mandate

Adjacent capabilities offered only in service of the rating work, never standalone — store search visibility and reputation on related platforms. The recovery method itself is private, walked through under NDA.
