Rating Intelligence. RIENVOR continuously monitors rating trends and review signals to identify the issues exerting the most pressure on an app's reputation. Where recurring patterns emerge, founders receive practical observations that help reduce future negative-review inflow — in service of rating health, not as a standalone report.

Paid user acquisition across CPI, CPR, and CPA models, structured around action-based performance rather than install volume, alongside organic search visibility built for funded and growth-stage businesses. Both disciplines are grounded in execution experience, not frameworks.

How engagements are structured

Every engagement begins with a private diagnostic: a focused conversation to understand the context, the objective, and whether there is genuine fit. Scope, structure, and commercial terms are discussed from there. Engagements run as defined projects or ongoing retainers. There is no generic proposal stage and no pre-packaged service tier. The mandate is built around the specific problem.

On method

A fair question at this point is how the rating work is actually done. The method is deliberately not described on this site — it is commercially sensitive, and it is walked through openly, under NDA, in the diagnostic conversation. What can be said here: the mandate is the rating layer itself — review-signal diagnosis, recovery above the threshold where installs convert, and ongoing maintenance. It does not require product changes to begin, and it is not crisis PR. Ask the question directly on the call; it gets a straight answer.
