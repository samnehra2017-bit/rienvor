Play Store and App Store ratings recovered above the line where installs convert, then held — with monthly rating intelligence. Direct founder oversight throughout.

Sub-4.0 ratings recovered & held · Managed at 100K+ review scale · Founder-led oversight

For consumer apps and funded businesses where the Play Store rating directly affects install conversion, acquisition cost, and investor confidence. Mandate scope runs from a baseline rating audit through to sustained, ongoing management. Not crisis PR: structural work on the rating layer.

  • Baseline rating audit: where the rating stands, what is dragging it, and the gap to the install-conversion line
  • App-store ratings recovery and review-base management — Google Play and App Store
  • Continuous maintenance: holding the rating at target month over month, at any review scale
  • Review-response strategy and feedback-intelligence systems
  • Rating stabilisation ahead of fundraising or M&A, where storefront trust shapes investor confidence

Every ongoing engagement runs on a monthly read of where the rating stands and what is moving it — so the product team works on what actually moves the number, not noise.

  • Rating health: where the rating moved over the month, and why
  • Complaint themes ranked by rating impact, with the corrections most likely to reduce negative inflow
  • Competitor rating and review-signal benchmarking — where you stand against comparable-scale apps
  • Category and positioning context relevant to the active mandate

The free Rating Check previews this read; clients get it every month, tracked over time.

Where a mandate calls for it, a few adjacent capabilities are available — only in service of the rating work, never as standalone tracks: store search visibility, and reputation on adjacent platforms such as Google Business Profile, Trustpilot, and Glassdoor.
