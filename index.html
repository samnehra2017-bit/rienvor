What we deliver

Recovering, stabilising, and maintaining the Play Store rating.

The rating layer treated as the unit of work — separate from the product itself. Review-signal analysis identifies what is dragging the rating, the erosion is arrested, and the number is recovered above the threshold where installs convert, then maintained so gains hold rather than drift. Open the ratings recovery & management detail to go deeper.

Ongoing engagements include a monthly rating-health report: complaint themes ranked by rating impact, with the corrections most likely to reduce negative inflow.

Two supporting disciplines, available where they serve the rating mandate.

User acquisition structured around downstream economics rather than install volume — grounded in execution at scale, not theory. Search visibility treated as a compounding asset rather than a campaign — built to hold as the market shifts rather than spike and fade.

Who this is for and how we work

Founders bring RIENVOR in when a rating has slipped below 4.0 and started costing installs, when negative reviews are arriving faster than positive ones, or when no one internally has the time to actively manage it. Engagements are private, direct, and founder-led.

What happens after you engage

  1. 01

    The first call

    Discovery

    A private conversation to understand the app, the pressure the rating is under, and whether there is genuine fit. No pitch, no obligation.

  2. 02

    Week one

    Diagnostic

    The review base is read closely, separating what is actually dragging the rating from noise, and a baseline is set. That read is yours whether or not we go further.

  3. 03

    Month one, then the months after

    Recovery

    The decline is arrested first: the rating is carried back to 4.0 quickly to stop the daily loss, then lifted steadily toward a 4.2–4.5 target over the months that follow.

  4. 04

    As the target is reached

    Stabilisation

    The gain is locked in so it holds rather than snaps back — review quality kept ahead of acquisition so the new floor is real, not a spike.

  5. 05

    Ongoing

    Maintenance

    The rating is held within a healthy band against ongoing negative pressure, tuned to your goals and budget, with the monthly rating-health report keeping the read current.

Not a theoretical practice. RIENVOR is built on work across 70+ Play Store apps and continues to actively manage rating health for consumer applications today.
