What we deliver

The rating is the unit of work — treated separately from the product itself.

  • Identify — review-signal analysis isolates what is actually dragging the rating down.
  • Arrest — the daily loss is stopped and the decline is reversed.
  • Recover — the rating is carried back above the 4.0 line, where installs convert.
  • Maintain — the gain is held month over month, not left to drift.

Structural work, not crisis PR — built to hold rather than spike. Go deeper →

Every engagement runs on a monthly read of where the rating stands and what is moving it.

  • Rating health — the number, the trend, and where it sits against your target band.
  • Complaint themes, ranked — what is dragging the rating, ordered by impact, with the fixes most likely to cut negative inflow.
  • Competitor position — where you stand against comparable-scale apps in your category.

The free Rating Check previews this read; clients get it every month, tracked over time.
