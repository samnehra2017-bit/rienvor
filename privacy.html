Privacy policy

Minimal data.
Maximum discretion.

RIENVOR is a boutique advisory practice, not a data business. We collect only what is necessary to respond to enquiries and deliver engagements. This document explains what that means in practice.

No data sold · No third-party marketing · Enquiries handled discreetly · NDA available on request

Last updated: May 2026. Governed by applicable data protection law in India. For questions, contact hello@rienvor.com.

What we collect

RIENVOR collects information only when you actively provide it. This includes:

  • Your name and email address, when submitted via the contact form or sent directly by email.
  • Any details about your business or enquiry that you choose to share.
  • Basic website analytics data — aggregated, anonymised, used only to understand site performance.

We do not collect sensitive personal data, payment information, or information about third parties without explicit cause.

Enquiry data

When you submit an enquiry — whether by form, email, or WhatsApp — the information you provide is used solely to respond to that enquiry and, if appropriate, to discuss a potential engagement. Enquiry data is not used for marketing, profiling, or any purpose outside the scope of your original communication.

All initial conversations are treated as confidential by default. If you request a non-disclosure agreement, one will be provided before the conversation deepens.

Analytics and cookies

This website may use basic analytics tools — such as Google Analytics — to understand how visitors interact with the site. These tools collect anonymised, aggregated data: page views, general device type, and referral sources. No personally identifiable information is collected through analytics.

Standard cookies may be set by these tools. You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time without affecting your ability to use the site.

Communications

RIENVOR does not send unsolicited marketing communications. If you have engaged with the practice and wish to receive updates, that will be agreed with you directly. You may withdraw from any communication at any time by emailing hello@rienvor.com.

Confidentiality

RIENVOR operates under a strict confidentiality standard. Client identities, engagement details, and any sensitive business information shared during enquiry or engagement are never disclosed to third parties, referenced publicly, or used for any purpose other than serving the engagement itself.

NDAs are available at first contact and are standard practice for engagements involving commercially sensitive information.

Data retention

We retain correspondence and enquiry information for as long as it is reasonably necessary — typically for the duration of an engagement and a reasonable period thereafter, in case of follow-up. If you would like your data removed, you may request this at any time and we will act promptly.

Third parties

RIENVOR does not sell, rent, or share personal data with third parties for commercial purposes. Data may pass through service infrastructure — such as email hosting or form processing tools — solely to enable communication. Such services are used under terms that restrict secondary use of the data they process.

Your rights

You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of any personal information we hold about you. You may also request that we restrict how your information is used. To exercise any of these rights, contact us directly at hello@rienvor.com. We will respond promptly.

Contact

For any questions about this policy or how RIENVOR handles your information, write to us at hello@rienvor.com. Direct communication is always the fastest and most reliable way to resolve any concern.
